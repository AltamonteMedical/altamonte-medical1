Premiere, physician-led internal medicine and primary care — designed around you

Altamonte Medical Associates, P.A. is a physician-led internal medicine and primary care practice serving Orlando and Altamonte Springs. We offer both insurance-based care and premiere care options for adults seeking more physician time, more thoughtful evaluation, and a more personalized healthcare experience.

Accepting New Patients Call today to speak directly with our office.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions.

Helpful information for patients considering care with Altamonte Medical Associates.

Are you accepting new patients?
Yes. We are welcoming new patients at Altamonte Medical Associates. Please call our office directly for the most current availability at each location, and our team will be happy to help guide you to the most appropriate office and appointment type.
Do you accept insurance?
We accept most major insurance plans, including Medicare. Please note that we do not participate with Medicaid.
Do you offer same-day appointments?
Same-day visits may be available depending on schedule and the nature of the concern. Please call the office directly for availability.
What services do you provide?
Our practice provides physician-led internal medicine, primary care, premiere care, preventive care, chronic disease management, telehealth, annual physicals, and medical weight management.
How do I schedule an appointment?
Appointments are scheduled directly by phone with our office during business hours to provide a more personal and efficient experience.
Do you offer premiere care options?
Yes. We offer premiere care options for adults seeking a more comprehensive, prevention-focused, physician-led evaluation with additional time and personalized attention.
Who is this practice best suited for?
Our model is ideal for adults who value a long-term physician relationship, preventive care, continuity, and a more thoughtful care experience.
Do you provide telehealth visits?
Yes. Telehealth may be available for appropriate follow-up and select non-emergency concerns depending on clinical needs.
What should I bring to my appointment?
Please bring a photo ID, insurance card, medication list, and any relevant records or recent test results that may help guide your care.
Do you treat chronic medical conditions?
Yes. We provide ongoing management of chronic conditions such as hypertension, diabetes, and lipid disorders, with an emphasis on continuity and prevention.
How do I know which office to call?
You may call either office location directly. Our team can help guide you to the most appropriate location based on availability and your needs.
